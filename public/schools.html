Questions

School route information for parents and schools.

How early should children arrive?

Children should be at their stop at least five minutes before the scheduled pickup time.

Do routes change?

Routes may be adjusted when traffic, demand or school requirements change. Families should follow current GLÁS updates.

Can another school be listed?

Yes. GLÁS can add future school pages with stops, times, return runs and booking links as routes are confirmed.
